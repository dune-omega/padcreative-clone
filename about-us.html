Helping brands to launch, evolve and tell their stories since 2002

An independent, established and highly creative, brand design and digital agency.

Back in 2002 and in their early 20s, Alison Steward and Peter Jacobs founded Pad Creative – armed only with some pencils, sketchbooks and a couple of beige Macs – in a tiny studio space in Beckenham, south London. Having worked together in a large London agency, they were convinced they could start a creative agency themselves and over 15 years – and many studio spaces – later, Pad is still going strong.

Pad has worked with some of its clients since those early days and from what they tell us, we’re a joy to work with – responsive, low on ego and able to ‘get’ what they are looking to achieve.

Not only is it a great agency to work with, it’s also a great agency to work for – we have a talented, close-knit team and a uniquely warm working atmosphere. We don’t want you dealing with “middlemen” account managers, so you’ll get direct access to the team of designers and developers actually working on your project.

Since the beginning, we’ve adopted a ‘right first time’ approach to our projects by taking a watertight design brief from the off. This lets us quote you a fixed price for the work you require, minimise any back and forth, and deliver to your deadline… not too shabby!
