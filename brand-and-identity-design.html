More about design

Your brand is a representation of your organisation’s values and ambitions. It’s at the heart of all your visual communications and needs to perform effectively. We create and develop brands that speak to your audience with clarity, placing you one step ahead of your competitors – increasing awareness, desirability, and vitally, sales of your products or services.

Whether you need to refine your existing identity or are starting from scratch, we’ll help you to increase your exposure to the market, launch new products and services, or reposition your identity to attract new types of customer.

If you’re a start-up, we’ll provide you with a visual identity that allows you to compete with the established players in your field. We will also deliver the tools to attract new business, build up a solid reputation and ensure an impactful arrival into your sector.

We’ll tailor a package to suit your needs, providing you with some or all of the following:

  • Name generation
  • Strapline writing
  • Logo design
  • Colour selection
  • Image and illustration designs
  • Fonts and typography treatment
  • Brand guidelines
