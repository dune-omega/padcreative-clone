More aboutreport design

A successful annual report is well thought out, engaging, accurate and beautifully designed. It represents your organisation to a wide range of stakeholders and must work hard to impress and inform.

Pad has a strong editorial design expertise, having produced a large number of annual reports over the last two
decades. We’ve designed reports for academic institutions, charities, housing associations, professional bodies and the public sector, as well as organisations in the architectural, arts and culture, financial, healthcare, and technology sectors.

Planned out

No matter how short your deadline, a well-honed production schedule is vital for the smooth running of your annual report design project. We’ll help you formulate a timetable, setting out roles and responsibilities for each stage and take ownership of the milestone deadlines to ensure you experience a smooth and punctual journey towards publication.

Accurate

Our processes and proofing stages will give you complete confidence in our work. To make certain your content and data is error-free, we work directly from your supplied files and avoid manual input at all costs.

Visually engaging

Creativity forms the foundation of everything we produce at Pad. Whether it’s visually-dynamic infographics, diagrams or charts to take the complicated and make it digestible; or clean, compliant and well-structured financial reporting, we’ll make sure your report is noticed for all the right reasons.

Printed and delivered

If you would like high quality printed copies of your report, we can handle the whole process for you. From paper stock selection through to fulfilment.

Available online

From a web-friendly PDF to a fully bespoke digital annual report, our design and web development teams can help you publish your report wherever you most need it.
